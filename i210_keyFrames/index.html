CSS Animations & Keyframes — I210

This page shows three animation concepts. Read the section title, look at the box, then check the CSS file to see how it works.

1. Fade In

The box starts at opacity: 0 (invisible) and ends at opacity: 1 (visible). The @keyframes rule named fadeIn controls this. It runs once when the page loads.

I fade in

2. Move (Translate)

translateX() moves the box left or right. At 0% it is at its normal spot. At 50% it has moved 200px to the right. At 100% it comes back. This loops forever.

I move left to right

3. Hover Effects

No @keyframes needed for hover. The transition property smoothly changes between the normal state and the :hover state. Move your mouse over each card.
